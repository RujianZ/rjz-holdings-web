zZuP! reframes campus social around accumulation rather than expression. The system observes three real-world signals — where you go, what you encounter, and who you build with — and translates them into accumulated titles, XP, and discovered landmarks. The output is a record of who someone has actually been in the world, not a feed of who they've claimed to be online.

The product is currently in pre-launch. The University of Pittsburgh pilot is the primary feedback loop into the launch build.
